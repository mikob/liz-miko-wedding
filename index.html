Frequently Asked Questions

Are children allowed?

Yes! We welcome your little ones and we expect many to bring their newborns, toddlers and kids!

What is the dress code?

Semi-formal attire is requested. The event is outdoors and the weather is expected to be chilly, so please dress warmly.

What should I bring if I'm traveling internationally?

At least your passport and a pen (to fill out customs forms). GDL airport is nearest!

What's the best way to get around?

Uber!

Can I bring a guest?

We have reserved seats for those named on your invitation. Please contact us if you have any questions.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, there is some parking available at the venue for guests but we recommend Uber'ing.

Is the water safe to drink?

Any water that is served or provided at restaurants or venues in Mexico comes from bottled sources. Do not drink water directly from the tap at your airbnb or hotel.
